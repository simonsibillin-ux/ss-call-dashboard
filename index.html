Scripts & Guidelines
Reference during calls
📞 Opening Every Call
"Thanks for calling SS Exterior Services, my name is [Name]. Before we get into it, could I grab your name and best contact number just in case we get cut off?"
🚨 Custom Quote — Escalation to Simon
"That's something that requires a custom quote — I'll pass your details straight to Simon who'll be in touch shortly. Could I grab your name, best contact number, and address? [If stains/photos needed: 'Could you also send through some photos to 0447 130 743?']"
🪟 Window Cleaning Disclaimer
"Just to let you know, this is a standard window clean and doesn't include removal of hard water stains, paint residue, silicone, or any heavy buildup. If there is any of that present on the day, we'll let you know and that would be quoted separately before we proceed."
🧪 Biocide Treatment Explanation
"A roof biocide treatment is a specialised chemical treatment we apply directly to your roof surface. It kills all existing lichen, moss, and algae at the root — not just on the surface — and prevents regrowth for up to 2 to 4 years depending on your roof and environment. You'll see the lichen gradually dying off and disappearing over the following months. It's a much gentler process than pressure washing, so it's completely safe for all roof types including terra cotta."
🐦 Bird Proofing Bundle Explanation
"Just so you know, our solar bird proofing is part of a complete service package. We include a full gutter clean and solar panel clean at the same time. That way the job is done to a complete standard — it also means you're not dealing with blocked gutters or dirty panels voiding any warranty on the proofing. It works out much better value all round."
🏡 House Washing — What's Included
"That includes a complete clean of your home's exterior — walls, facades, gutters, fascia, and eaves. It doesn't include the roof — that's a separate service if you need it. All our cleaning methods comply with manufacturer specifications for your building materials, so it's fully warranty compliant. We'll also send you before and after photos."
✅ Closing Every Call
"So just to confirm, we'll be doing [SERVICE] at [SUBURB] for a total of $[PRICE] including travel. That includes [KEY INCLUSIONS]. When works best for you — are you flexible on dates or is there a particular time that suits?"
